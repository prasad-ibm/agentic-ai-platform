Root Cause Analysis · 5 Forecasting Failures
Current State vs. Agentic AI Future State
Each pain point mapped to its AI agent solution and the measurable outcome it delivers.
PROBLEM #1
Segment / Legal Entity Mismatch (OPAM · Equistar)
HIGH
⚠ Current State
OPAM is largely the Equistar legal entity which spans O&P, APS, and I&D. Invoices and payables don't cleanly map to operating segments — creating a persistent disconnect between business activity and balance sheet visibility. Finance cannot reconcile segment performance against legal entity cash movements.
3
BUs impacted by entity mismatch
✓ Agentic AI Solution
Multi-Entity Mapper Agent maintains a continuously updated entity-to-segment resolution table. Every Equistar transaction is automatically attributed to O&P, APS, or I&D in real-time, giving Business Finance a clean segment-level cash view without manual reconciliation.
100%
Transaction-to-segment attribution
PROBLEM #2
Cost Allocation Opacity — No SAP Drill-Down
HIGH
⚠ Current State
Costs are allocated to segments but the Business Finance team has no visibility into timing or origin. SAP doesn't provide drill-down into what makes up an allocation. A $10MM HR invoice appears only when it posts — impossible to forecast overhead or inventory costs before they hit the books.
0%
Pre-posting cost allocation visibility
✓ Agentic AI Solution
Cost Allocation Tracer Agent connects to upstream cost center commitments, scheduled allocation runs, and HR payroll cycles — surfacing pending charges 30–45 days before they post to SAP GL. Finance sees what's coming, not just what's landed.
30–45d
Advance allocation visibility window
PROBLEM #3
Accounts Payable Black Box — Single Largest Problem
CRITICAL
⚠ Current State
AP balances flow directly into segment balance sheets with no forward visibility into what is due or when. This is the single largest forecasting failure across all segments. Without AP maturity data, the cash conversion cycle cannot be managed — only reacted to.
<10%
Invoice due-date visibility in AP
✓ Agentic AI Solution
AP Maturity Agent reads every open invoice across all segments, maps payment terms and aging buckets, and auto-generates a rolling 13-week AP cash requirements schedule broken out by segment, vendor category, and due-date band. Gives O&P Finance the visibility they need to proactively manage cash.
13-wk
Rolling AP cash requirements schedule
PROBLEM #4
CapEx Payment Shock Events — $200MM vs $50MM Forecast
CRITICAL
⚠ Current State
$200MM was paid in a single month against a $50MM forecast — multiple CapEx invoices clustered due at the same time with no advance warning. The team had no visibility into CapEx payable balances or when those payables became due. A 4× forecast miss with direct P&L and Treasury impact.
4×
CapEx forecast miss (Feb example)
✓ Agentic AI Solution
CapEx Invoice Monitor tracks every open CapEx payable across all projects, models due-date clustering risk, and issues automated alerts when projected monthly CapEx cash exceeds 120% of plan — with a minimum 30-day lead time. The February $200MM event would have triggered an alert 32 days in advance.
32d
Advance CapEx clustering alert (Feb scenario)
PROBLEM #5
Treasury Cash Inflow Overstatement & Liquidity Risk
HIGH
⚠ Current State
Treasury relies on forecasted cash inflows to plan creditor payments, dividends, and other obligations. When the working capital forecast overstates expected cash generation, Treasury is forced to reposition cash at short notice — creating liquidity risk, operational complexity, and increased cost of carry.
High
Treasury liquidity reposition risk
✓ Agentic AI Solution
Cash Inflow Predictor Agent cross-references AR aging, customer payment behaviour history, and macro credit signals to build probability-weighted inflow forecasts. Automatically flags overstatement risk to Treasury with confidence intervals — converting a reactive function to a proactive one.
±3%
Target cash inflow forecast variance
💡
Scope recommendation (from working session): Begin with short-term forecasting — 13-week rolling working capital — focused on O&P, targeting Problems #3 (AP) and #4 (CapEx) first as highest dollar impact. Extend to long-range planning (LRP) and scenario modelling in Phase 2. Data elements are shared across horizons — foundational agents built in Phase 1 scale directly to LRP. Involve I&D and APS in interview phase to support future scaling. Segment involvement confirmed: O&P primary, I&D and APS in discovery.
