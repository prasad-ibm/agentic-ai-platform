The Shift
From annual spreadsheet marathons to always-on planning intelligence
Every FP&A role evolves. Manual model builds, data wrangling, and once-a-year planning cycles disappear. What remains is what humans are uniquely good at: judgment, strategy, narrative, and governance.
01
Modellers become Curators
FP&A shifts from building models in Excel to curating AI-generated scenarios. People add judgment; agents do the computation.
02
Annual Plans become Continuous
Rolling forecasts refresh as business conditions change. Agents update assumptions, reforecast, and alert on drift — no more once-a-year planning sprints.
03
Reporting becomes Insight
Dashboards give way to narrative-ready packages. Agents pinpoint cost drivers, margin leakage, and capital allocation opportunities automatically.
