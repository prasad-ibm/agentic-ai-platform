Bottom Line
Today's AI coding tools are excellent tactical accelerators — compressing hours to minutes on well-scoped tasks. They are not yet autonomous enterprise engineering platforms. The critical gaps are: persistent memory, closed-loop execution, governance controls, and verified reasoning on proprietary systems. Closing these gaps requires a deliberate Agentic Orchestration layer — not just point tools.
