The Shift
From month-end firefighters to supervisors of continuous close
Every R2R role evolves. Manual journals, recon chases, and late nights at close disappear. What remains is what humans are uniquely good at: policy, judgment, narrative, and assurance.
01
From month-end to continuous
Agents post, reconcile, and certify in-flight. The close is a moment, not a sprint — and flux is understood day-one.
02
From preparers to policy owners
CAOs and controllers define rules; agents execute at scale. Humans govern standards, review judgment, and sign certifications.
03
From reports to narratives
AI produces the statements; the finance team produces the insight, story, and disclosure that audiences actually need.
