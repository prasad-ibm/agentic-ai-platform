The Thesis
Three shifts that define the agentic finance organization

Across O2C and R2R, the same pattern repeats — people move up the value chain while agents do the volume work under governed policy.

◉ Shift 01 · Role
Doers become supervisors
Every persona shifts from processing transactions to supervising agents, investigating exceptions, and teaching the system new patterns. People own judgment; agents own throughput.
◉ Shift 02 · Time
Reactive becomes predictive
Instead of closing what happened, finance anticipates what will. Revenue leakage is caught at origination, impairment indicators surface before events, disputes are prevented before raised.
◉ Shift 03 · Shape
Silos become orchestration
O2C and R2R stop being functional handoffs and become a single orchestrated network. Policy deployed once applies everywhere. Evidence is continuous. Close is a live position, not a monthly event.
