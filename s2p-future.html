The Shift
From tactical order-takers to supervisors of autonomous procurement
Every S2P role evolves. Tactical PO entry, manual supplier chasing, and reactive risk management disappear. What remains is what humans do best: strategy, judgment, and policy governance.
01
Buyers become Strategists
Procurement shifts from tactical PO entry to sourcing strategy and supplier development. Agents govern rules, run RFQs, and handle routine buying.
02
Reactive becomes Predictive
Supplier risk, category spend, and payment anomalies are detected before they escalate. Agents monitor signals continuously across every supplier.
03
Silos become Orchestration
Sourcing, AP, and compliance become a single agentic network. Policy deployed once applies across all touchpoints — no more disconnected handoffs.
