The Shift
From transaction processors to supervisors of autonomous operations
Every role in the O2C lifecycle evolves. Manual data entry and exception chasing disappear. What remains are the uniquely human capabilities: judgment, strategy, relationship, and policy design.
01
From doers to supervisors
Agents run the routine. Humans handle exceptions, policy, and strategy — the 15% where judgment lives.
02
From reactive to predictive
AI forecasts disputes, late payments, and revenue leakage before they happen. Intervention replaces firefighting.
03
From silos to orchestration
Order, billing, revenue, credit, collections, and cash operate as one connected agentic workflow — not seven disconnected teams.
